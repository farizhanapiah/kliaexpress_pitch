The Experience Design
What It Looks, Feels,
and Moves Like
01
Zone 01 · KL Sentral Concourse
"Gateway of Malaysia"
Projection Arc
A large-format projection-mapped archway displaying a real-time visual journey through Malaysia's 63 years. Motion-reactive: footsteps trigger ripples in the visuals as passengers walk through it.
02
Zone 02 · KL Sentral + KLIA
MimpiLab AI
Photobooth Station
2–3 units at arrivals/departures. Choose a Malaysia Day theme — National Hero, Cultural Icon, Future Malaysian — and receive a branded AI-generated photo in under 30 seconds.
03
Zone 03 · Key Dwell Zones
Hoomans.ai
Interactive Host
A holographic AI avatar in national colours on an 86" display. Multilingual across BM, English, Mandarin. Shares Malaysia Day trivia, KLIA Express history, drives photobooth participation.
04
Zone 04 · Social Amplification
Live Social
Amplification Wall
Real-time display of Malaysia Day photos taken at the station with #PerjalananKita and #KLIAExpress. Creates a feedback loop of pride and social shareability.
